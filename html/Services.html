Medical Staff Outsourcing

Our medical staff outsourcing services provide highly skilled and compassionate healthcare professionals to support your home care needs. We offer a diverse team of registered nurses, licensed practical nurses, certified nursing assistants, physical therapists, and occupational therapists who are dedicated to delivering personalized care. Our rigorous screening and training processes ensure that our staff are not only qualified but also committed to enhancing the well-being of our clients.

Registered Nurses (RNs): Provide skilled nursing care, administer medications, manage chronic conditions, and perform advanced medical procedures.

Licensed Practical Nurses (LPNs): Offer essential nursing care, monitor vital signs, and assist with daily living activities.

Certified Nursing Assistants (CNAs): Assist with personal care tasks such as bathing, dressing, and mobility.

Therapists: Deliver physical, occupational, and speech therapy to aid in rehabilitation and recovery.

Equipment Supplies

Our home care equipment supplies ensure that you have access to the necessary tools and devices to support your health and independence at home. We offer a wide range of high-quality medical equipment designed to enhance comfort, safety, and mobility.

Mobility Aids: Walkers, canes, and wheelchairs to assist with movement and prevent falls.

Respiratory Devices: Oxygen concentrators, CPAP machines, and nebulizers for respiratory support.

Patient Care Equipment: Hospital beds, patient lifts, and commodes for comfort and convenience.

Monitoring Devices:  Blood pressure monitors, glucose meters, and pulse oximeters to keep track of vital health metrics.

Our dedicated team is available to help you select the right equipment and provide guidance on its use, ensuring that you receive the full benefits of these essential supplies.

Medical Transportation

Astute Tech Medical Solutions offers reliable medical transportation services to ensure safe and comfortable travel for medical appointments, treatments, and other healthcare needs. Our transportation solutions are designed to accommodate various medical conditions and mobility challenges, providing peace of mind for patients and their families.

Non-Emergency Medical Transportation (NEMT): Safe and efficient transportation for routine medical visits, therapy sessions, and outpatient procedures.

Ambulance Services:  For patients requiring specialized medical care during transport, our ambulances are equipped with advanced life-support systems and staffed by trained medical personnel.

Wheelchair-Accessible Vehicles: Ensuring that individuals with mobility impairments can travel comfortably and safely to their destinations.

Remote Patient Monitoring

Our remote patient monitoring (RPM) services utilize advanced technology to track and manage patients' health conditions from a distance. RPM allows healthcare providers to monitor vital signs, receive real-time updates, and intervene promptly when necessary.

Vital Signs Monitoring: Continuous tracking of key health metrics such as heart rate, blood pressure, and glucose levels

Real-Time Alerts: Immediate notifications sent to healthcare providers in case of abnormal readings.

Data Analytics: Comprehensive analysis of patient data to identify trends and make informed clinical decisions.

Patient Engagement: Tools and apps to help patients actively participate in their own care

Benefits of Remote Patient Monitoring:

Enhanced patient care through proactive health management

Reduced hospital readmissions and emergency room visits

Improved management of chronic conditions
